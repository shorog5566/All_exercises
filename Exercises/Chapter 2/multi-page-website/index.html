Quick Overview

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film that revolutionized blockbuster cinema. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio, the film follows Dom Cobb on a dangerous journey through multiple layers of dreams. With a budget of $160 million and box office earnings of $839.3 million worldwide, Inception proved that intelligent, complex narratives could still captivate global audiences.

The film won 4 Academy Awards and remains a benchmark for innovative storytelling and visual effects in modern cinema.
