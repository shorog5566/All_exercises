Main Characters

Dom Cobb

Portrayed by Leonardo DiCaprio

The protagonist and a skilled extractor who specializes in stealing secrets from people's subconscious while they dream. Dom is haunted by the memory of his wife and desperate to return to his children. His moral ambiguity, struggles with guilt, and complex psychology make him one of cinema's most compelling characters. Throughout the film, he battles both external threats and his own internal demons.

Mal Cobb

Portrayed by Marion Cotillard

Dom's deceased wife who exists as a projection in his subconscious mind. Mal represents his guilt, regret, and inability to move forward from the past. She is intelligent, manipulative, and dangerous, sabotaging the team's missions. Her character serves as a manifestation of Cobb's inner turmoil and becomes increasingly antagonistic as the team delves deeper into the dream layers.

Ariadne

Portrayed by Ellen Page

A young, brilliant architecture student recruited by Cobb to design the dream worlds for the inception mission. Ariadne is curious, intelligent, and initially naive about the moral implications of dream-sharing. She becomes the moral center of the team, gradually discovering the truth about Cobb's past and questioning the ethics of their mission. Her character arc represents redemption and standing up for what is right.

Arthur

Portrayed by Joseph Gordon-Levitt

Cobb's right-hand man and a calm, calculating dream-share expert. Arthur is competent, logical, and loyal to Cobb despite his flaws. He serves as the team's technical expert and provides crucial support throughout the mission. Arthur represents stability and reliability, often serving as the voice of reason within the group, though he struggles with the moral implications of their work.

Eames

Portrayed by Tom Hardy

A skilled forger who can impersonate anyone within the dream world. Eames is charismatic, confident, and has a knack for languages. Despite his criminal background, he brings levity and humor to the team. His loyalty to Cobb and willingness to improvise make him invaluable to the mission. Eames represents the rebel element within the group.

Robert Fischer

Portrayed by Cillian Murphy

The target of the inception mission, a corporate heir whose mind the team must infiltrate. Fischer is initially depicted as the antagonist's tool, but over the course of the film, he becomes sympathetic. His journey through the dream layers reveals layers of complexity, as he grapples with his relationship with his father and his own identity. He becomes a reluctant ally by the film's end.

Yusuf

Portrayed by Dileep Rao

A brilliant chemist who creates powerful sedatives that allow the team to enter and maintain deep dream states. Yusuf's pharmaceutical expertise is crucial to the mission's success. He is pragmatic, somewhat cynical, and serves as the team's chemist and driver in the dream levels. His sedative is so powerful that time moves even more slowly in those dreams.

Saito

Portrayed by Ken Watanabe

A powerful businessman who employs Cobb and funds the inception mission. Saito is calculating, wealthy, and has his own agenda. He becomes trapped in the dream world and must be rescued by the team. His presence adds an additional layer of complexity to the mission, as his survival becomes vital to everyone's escape from the dream layers.
